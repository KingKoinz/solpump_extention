## Truth About "Prediction"

‚ö†Ô∏è **Important Reality Check:**

Even the **best AI can't reliably beat crash games** because:
- Results are provably fair (cryptographically random)
- House has mathematical edge
- Past patterns don't guarantee future results

**What AI CAN do:**
- Identify short-term statistical anomalies
- Optimize cashout timing
- Reduce variance in your betting
- Help you avoid obvious bad bets

**What AI CAN'T do:**
- Guarantee profits
- "Beat the system"
- Predict truly random events

**Bottom line:** AI helps you play smarter, not win guaranteed.
